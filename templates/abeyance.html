Abeyance

Abeyance is a J-style RPG I started a long time ago and intend to finish it.

You take control of a man named "Keats" who lives in an Orwellian dystopia. Except Keats suffers from a number of delusions and is gradually finding reality and dream blending.

The project was originally started in Dark Basic Professional and the first demo was made. A Unity3D demo followed, but now, it is back in development.

It will be developed in Unity3D again. But it will be taking advantage of a few things available from the asset store.

This will include media and ORK as well as a map editor.

Game Play

The gameplay is based on a turn-based RPG battle system. It was built out of my aspiration to make such an RPG. However, I have introduced a problem solving aspect to it.

Throughout areas you will find clues to get out. These may be clues on how to defeat the boss, or to open a door. Some will be complicated and may even need a pen and paper.

For instance, the original demo enticed you to learn a bit of Old Norse to find the boss's weakness.

To accomodate for this, some fights may prove to be challenging. As I wish for the player to focus on tactics and not grinding levels and learning overpowered skills. This is why it is a turn-based RPG, to allow the player to consider their moves wisely. I may look to some inspiration from XCOM here.

Thresholds

Most RPG's will have elements. We have Thresholds.

  • Curse
  • Blood
  • Frantic
  • Nerve
  • Fear
  • Empowerment
  • Lucid

    • They will not just work like elements do, they will also provide other benefits to you. For example, Blood will make you stronger.

    More about Thresholds

    As the game is focused around the mental state of Keats, he will be able to maintain his own Threshold.

    As the player, you will be able to change Keats' threshold to "Nerve" and get a boost to Nerve attacks, an advantage to Curse ones and a disadvantage to Frantic ones. But also get an accuracy boost.
